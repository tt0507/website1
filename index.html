OPEN YOKOHAMA

Yokohama, the second largest city in Japan, served as the entrace of modern urbanization of Japan during the Meiji Era. The adaptation of Yokohama from Japanese culture to a mix of European and Japanese culture shaped the building landscape and culture of Yokohama.
