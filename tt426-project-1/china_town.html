China Town

The China Town located in central Yokohama is the largest China Town located in Japan. Many chinese restaurants and stores can be found in the area. Various events are held throughtout the year including Chinese New Years which is held around Feburary. The main attraction of China Town is the variety of Chinese cuisine offered. Visitors can find steamed buns, ramen noddles which tastes are arranged for Japanese people, or can eat authentic Chinese food at restaurants located throughout the area.
