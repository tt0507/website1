Minato Mirai 21

Located near the harbor, Yokohama-kou, Minato Mirai 21 is a urban area located at Yokohama. Minato Mirai offers tourists with plenty of attractions including shopping center, hotels, amusement parks, museums, and a onsen (hot-spring) center where tourists relax.

Entertainment

Ranging from relaxed onsen center to getting involved in large-scale events at Aka-Renga Soko, Minato Mirai offers a variet of entertainment options.

Cosmo World

The cosmo World consists of the famous ferris wheel with roller coasters and plenty of arcade games. At night the whole park is illuminated, adding on to the beautiful night view scenery.

Aka Renga Soko (Red Brick Warehouse)

Used as port during the Meiji era, Aka Renga has changed into a symbol of Minato Mirai. Two buildings have a number of shopping venues and restaurants located. Various events are held according to the season; the skate rink being the most famous.

Manyo Club

The Manyo club is a onsen facility which offers services such as massage, restaurants, relaxation rooms, and a hot spring bath. Manyo club is the ideal place to rest your body after a long day at Minato Mirai.

Cup Noodle Museum

Visitors follow the history of cup noodles with through interactive and hands-on experience. You can also create your own customized cup noodle.

Shopping Center

Landmark Plaza

Located beside the Landmark Tower, the Landmark Plaza offers a variety of shops and restaurants.

Queen's Square

Queen's Square is located at the buttom of the Queen's Tower, offering a variety of shops and restaurants for tourists and visitors.
