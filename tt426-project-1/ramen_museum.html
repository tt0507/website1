Ramen Museum

The Shinyokohama ramen museum is located at Shinyokohama, around 20~30 minutes away from Minato Mirai. As the name states, the museum is dedicated to various types of ramen and its history. The first floor of the museum presents the history of the ramen noodles in Japan. There are displays of the variety of noodles eaten throughout the country and shows how they are made. History on the instant noodles are also present. On the two basement floors, visitors can find replicas of the town of Shitamachi, old town of Tokyo, when ramen was booming.Nine ramen restaurtants can be found in which visitors can enjoy ramen from different regions of Japan.
