Spring

Yamashita Park/Minato-no-Mieru-Oka Park & Aka-Renga

Visitors can come look at the beautiful flowers that are on display during the spring. During March and April, there will also be sakuras in each area.

Summer

Hanabi(fireworks)

Throughout the summer, there will be fireworks along with the Natsumatsuri (summer festival) help at various parts at Yokohama. The firework at the Osanbashi pier at Minato Mirai consists of beautiful patterns and character illustrations.

Asahi Jazz Festival

The Asahi Jazz festival is a special one-day stage where numerous Jazz groups perform.

Pikachu Parade

An army of pikachu walks accross Minato-Mirai including Queen's Square and Landmark Plaza. There are Pikachu related events that are followed after the march.

Autumn

October Festival

Visitors can drink all types of beer while enjoying music and food for Germany

Bread Festival

Bakery shops from all over the country will gather for this special occasion to provide visitors with their bakery products.

Winter

Skate Rink

From December to Feburary, visitors can enjoy skating on the skate rink.
